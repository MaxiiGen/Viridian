About Viridian

Viridian helps farmers and growers access reliable agricultural supplies while making sustainable practices easier to adopt in everyday operations.

Our Mission

Build a practical, trusted marketplace where quality farm inputs are accessible, transparent, and backed by useful guidance.

Our Vision

Empower local agriculture through technology, responsible sourcing, and stronger connections between growers and suppliers.

What We Value

  • Product quality and authenticity
  • Farmer-first support and education
  • Sustainable and climate-aware farming

IoT Services

Monitor your farm with connected tools that help you track moisture, irrigation, and crop conditions in real time.

Smart Irrigation

Automate watering schedules based on sensor readings to avoid overwatering and reduce wastage.

Crop Monitoring

Get visibility into field status with regular device updates that help you respond faster to issues.

Usage Analytics

Review historical trends and optimize your setup with data-backed insights over time.
